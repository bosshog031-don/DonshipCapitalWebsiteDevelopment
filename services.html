01 Business Credit + Funding
We build your full credit profile from scratch — EIN, DUNS, trade lines, and funding strategy. We identify the right sources, stage your applications, and position you to be fundable. Starter includes 3 trade lines. Growth gets 5+. Scale gets advanced funding: LOC stacking, SBA prep, and more.
02 E-Commerce Store
Full store buildout — product listings, Stripe payments, checkout optimization, abandoned cart flows, and up to 20 products. Ready to sell on day one. We handle the platform, the design, the payments, and the basic email flows so you can focus on your products.
03 Marketing System
Content calendar, email marketing sequences, lead capture, and ad setup. Your audience-building and customer acquisition machine, running from day one. Includes welcome sequences, lead magnet delivery, and a structured content plan for your first 90 days.
04 AI Operations (VoiceDesk + LeadCatch)
AI receptionist that answers every call, qualifies callers, and books appointments — 24/7 with no staff required. Plus AI chat deployed across your site, WhatsApp, and SMS so no lead ever falls through. Never miss a call or a message again.
05 FlowEngine + PocketOps Automation
Custom automation layer connecting your CRM, store, and tools so data moves without you touching it. Plus a mobile AI agent handling your scheduling, follow-ups, and daily ops around the clock. Included in Scale — available as add-ons for other packages.
